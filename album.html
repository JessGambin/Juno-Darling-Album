About the Album

"This album is very near and dear to my heart. I've been working hard on it for a for at least 3 years now with the help of my amazing team and producers. It's taken so long but, I wanted to make sure all of the songs invovled were at their absolute best for those who listen. I don't just want to provide you all with nice songs, I want them to be AMAZING. Chasin' a Moment is an album that will make you cry, dance, sing, and feel. I sincerly hope you like this album as much as I do. I'm super proud of this one. Have a Darling Day!"

Love, Juno
