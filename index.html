About Juno

Juno Darling is a 23 year old jazz/swing singer and songwriter. As a child she was constantly inspired by the works of Frank Sinatra and Dean Martin. She aspired to produce music that felt like magic just as these well-known artists once did. Juno started singing and writing songs at a very young age and made this beloved hobbie into a career. She hopes to inspire other people with her music the way her music idols have. Juno has been making music professionally for 4 years now making singles as well as one album prior to her newest one. Her new album "Chasin' a Dream" is out on December 11th. It's worth every penny. And in the words of Juno "Have another Darling day!"
